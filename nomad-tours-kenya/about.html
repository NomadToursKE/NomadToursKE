About Us

Discover our story, mission, and values

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of passionate Kenyan adventurers, Nomad Tours Kenya was born from a deep love for our country's breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage.

What started as a small group of friends leading weekend hikes in the Aberdare Ranges has grown into a full-fledged adventure company offering curated experiences across Kenya's most stunning destinations. Our founders, having explored every corner of this beautiful country, wanted to share their passion with both local and international travelers.

Today, we're proud to be one of Kenya's leading adventure tour operators, known for our commitment to sustainable tourism and authentic cultural experiences.

Our Mission

To provide unforgettable, sustainable travel experiences that showcase Kenya's natural beauty and cultural richness while supporting local communities and preserving the environment for future generations.

Our Vision

To be the leading sustainable adventure tourism company in East Africa, known for creating meaningful connections between travelers, local communities, and the natural world.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices that minimize our environmental impact and preserve Kenya's natural beauty.

Community

We empower local communities by creating employment opportunities and supporting local businesses.

Safety

Your well-being is our top priority. We maintain the highest safety standards on all our tours.

Excellence

We're dedicated to providing exceptional service and creating memorable experiences for our guests.
