Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours, bookings, and travel requirements.

Kenya is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit is during the dry seasons: from late June to October (great for wildlife viewing) and from December to March (ideal for beach holidays and the wildebeest migration in the Maasai Mara).

Most visitors to Kenya require a visa, which can be obtained online through the e-Visa portal or on arrival at the airport. Requirements vary by nationality, so please check with your local Kenyan embassy or consulate before traveling.

We recommend packing:

  • Neutral-colored clothing (khaki, beige, olive green)
  • Lightweight, breathable fabrics
  • Warm layers for early morning game drives
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sun protection (hat, sunglasses, sunscreen)
  • Binoculars and camera
  • Any necessary medications

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the tour package and time of cancellation. Generally, cancellations made more than 30 days before departure receive a full refund, while cancellations within 30 days may incur fees. Please refer to the specific terms and conditions of your booking or contact us for more information.
